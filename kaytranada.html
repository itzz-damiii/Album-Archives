Timeless

by KAAYTRANADA

Why I Like This Album

FKaytranada's "Timeless" album is one of my favorites for a variety of reasons!!! Sometimes I wanna feel funky, but not in a "cringe" type of way. I just wanna be able to dance, and feel groovy, and maybe even shocked by how unexpected the musical elements of a song are. Even though he's not a singer, he makes you feel as badass, confident, or even emotionally connected as he'd want you to be. Sometimes I even go bakc and put his songs on repeat just to see if i could discover something I hadn't noticed before. That, is why I love this album. Expect the unexpected.
