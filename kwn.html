With All Due Respect

by kwn

Why I Like This Album

Fun fact, I've been singing since I was six! I've had my dabble in songwriting. It hasn't been the best but I'll get there. KWN's "With All Due Respect" felt like it brought to life the vibe I always wanted when I was in chorus. We may have sung the harmonies, the melodies, but it felt like they were lacking life in the way I wanted them. Growing up in a Nigerian household, going to a Nigerian church, music was central to the way we wanted to worship and honor God, and connect emotionally beyond words. Singing was my first love, but before I left chorus, it felt like the love was no longer there. KWN's use of choruses (and I'm not talking about the ones you'd just hear in the background, they were VIBRANT) reignited my love of unity within music. That's why I love this album so much.
